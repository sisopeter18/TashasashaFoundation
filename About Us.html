Who We Are

We came up with Tashasashafoundation on 23 August 2023.... . This has been a dream of ours for a long time, and we are determined to make a positive impact in the lives of orphans. Our ultimate goal is to establish a children's home in the near future. We believe that every child deserves a loving and nurturing environment to thrive in, and we want to be the foundation that provides just that. Through @tashasashafoundation, we aim to provide support, care, and opportunities for orphans. We want to ensure that they have access to education, healthcare, and emotional support. We believe that by investing in their well-being, we can help shape a brighter future for these children.

Our Mission

To nurture the potential of young people, particularly those in orphanages, by providing essential resources, fostering personal growth, and promoting community engagement through impactful programs, mentorship, and serviceÂ initiatives.

Our Vision

A world where every young person, especially those facing adversity, feels empowered, supported, and equipped to thrive, contributing to a brighter future for their communities.

Our Goals

Education Access

Provide quality education to 1 million children in underserved communities by 2025 through school construction, teacher training, and scholarship programs.

Healthcare Improvement

Establish 50 community health centers and provide medical care to 500,000 people annually by 2024, focusing on maternal and child health.

Clean Water Initiative

Bring clean water and sanitation to 300 communities by 2023, reducing waterborne diseases and improving quality of life.

Sustainable Agriculture

Train 10,000 farmers in sustainable agricultural practices by 2025, increasing food security and economic stability in rural areas.

Housing Projects

Build 1,000 affordable homes for homeless families by 2026, providing safe and stable living environments.

Economic Empowerment

Create 5,000 microloans and vocational training opportunities annually to help individuals start small businesses and gain financial independence.
